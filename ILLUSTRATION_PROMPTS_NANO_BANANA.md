# Consultin illustration prompts for Nano Banana

Use these prompts to generate production illustrations for Consultin.

Goal: premium business-intelligence web app for Indonesian MSMEs. Not AI startup slop. Not cute mascot. Not generic SaaS cards.

Output format:
- Generate one image per prompt.
- Aspect ratio: 16:9 unless prompt says otherwise.
- Export final as `.webp`.
- Keep empty safe areas where UI text may overlay.
- No text inside images unless prompt explicitly says tiny unreadable report marks.
- No watermark, no Gemini/Nano Banana mark, no fake logo.

Brand palette:
- Primary blue: `#2A74C4`
- Deep navy: `#14385E`
- App ink: `#102B46`
- Dark mode base: `#071321`
- Dark card: `#0D1E33`
- Light background: `#F3F7FC`
- White surface: `#FFFFFF`
- Soft blue: `#6EA8D8`
- Pale text/surface: `#EAF4FF`, `#F7FBFF`
- Success green, only tiny accents: `#2D7568`

Visual direction:
- Mood: fintech-grade, consultant dossier, operational command room, credible Indonesian business analysis.
- Materials: matte navy panels, crisp white report paper, frosted blue glass only when subtle, thin hairline grids, paper dossier layers, annotated evidence cards.
- Lighting: controlled office light, soft blue ambient glow, no neon nightclub glow.
- Composition: asymmetric, editorial, dense but organized.
- Texture: subtle paper grain or technical grid allowed.
- Typography implied: Plus Jakarta Sans / Poppins / Montserrat-like, but avoid readable text.

Hard bans:
- no purple AI gradient
- no floating robot
- no mascot
- no sparkles
- no hologram brain
- no generic orb blob
- no smiling corporate stock team
- no rocket
- no 3D cartoon character
- no fake readable UI text
- no random crypto dashboard
- no futuristic sci-fi cockpit
- no over-glossy glassmorphism
- no equal three-card SaaS template

Negative prompt to append to every generation:
`generic AI startup, purple gradient, glowing robot, mascot, sparkles, floating orb, meaningless blobs, cartoon 3D character, unreadable clutter, fake readable text, watermark, logo, Gemini watermark, random neon, stock photo team, childish illustration, crypto trading UI, sci-fi spaceship, messy composition, low contrast, oversaturated colors`

## Generation checklist

Before accepting generated image:
- Palette follows brand blues and navy.
- No purple AI glow.
- No mascot/robot/sparkle.
- No readable fake text.
- Composition has useful negative space for UI.
- Looks like consultant/fintech/business intelligence, not generic startup.
- Works in both dark/light app context or prompt-specific mode.
- No watermark.
