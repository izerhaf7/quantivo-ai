# Product

## Register

product

## Platform

web

## Users

Indonesian UMKM founders and small-business operators who need to validate business ideas, understand local markets, compare competitors, and turn incomplete assumptions into practical decisions. They may not know how to write a formal business brief, so the product must accept messy natural-language input and guide them toward usable analysis.

## Product Purpose

Consultin turns a founder's rough business idea into a structured analysis workflow: chat intake, brief decomposition, follow-up questions, agent processing, confidence scoring, summary, full report, and slide deck output for higher tiers. Success means the user trusts the analysis process, understands what data is missing, and receives clear next actions rather than generic AI text.

## Brand Personality

Precise, calm, credible. Consultin should feel like an analyst team that explains its assumptions, checks missing information, and presents evidence clearly. The voice is Indonesian-first, plain, direct, and practical; it avoids hype, emojis, mascot energy, and vague promises.

## Anti-references

Do not make the product feel like a generic chatbot, decorative SaaS dashboard, mascot onboarding, glassmorphism AI app, purple-gradient landing page, or cute founder coach. Avoid cartoon business characters, emoji-driven UI, vague “AI magic” language, and reports that look impressive but hide confidence, assumptions, or evidence quality.

## Design Principles

1. Show the work: expose extracted brief fields, missing information, agent tasks, evidence counts, confidence levels, and assumptions.
2. Guide before spending quota: the user should review and complete the brief before agent processing begins.
3. Make trust scannable: priority, risk, confidence, evidence quality, and next action must be visible before detailed charts.
4. Keep the interface task-first: density is acceptable when it helps decisions; decoration must serve orientation or confidence.
5. Tier-gate outputs transparently: summary can be free, while full reports and slide decks are positioned as higher-tier artifacts without blocking understanding.

## Accessibility & Inclusion

Target WCAG AA for responsive web. Preserve keyboard navigation, visible focus states, 44px minimum touch targets, readable Indonesian copy, sufficient contrast for muted text and status colors, non-color-only status indicators, and reduced-motion alternatives for onboarding and agent pipeline transitions.
